artwork

To me, art is not just a representation of what we observe, but rather a deep understanding of people’s daily lives—their experiences, voices, and actions. My work today is mainly inspired by the baroque art period, where I aim to manipulate shadows and light in order to portray a vulnerability in a subject’s. I hope to reflect loneliness, serenity, human fragility, and the brevity of life all in a snapchat of time.
